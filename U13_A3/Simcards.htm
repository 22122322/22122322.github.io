Like a traditional SIM card, an eSIM stores the data your device needs to use the EE network. 'eSIM' simply means an embedded SIM, but unlike a physical SIM card, an eSIM is already built into your device. It also allows you to have multiple lines on a device, that you can switch between.The default SIM PIN is 1111. To change this: go to our Device Guides and select your device.

What Data is Stored on iPhone SIM Cards. Unlike some other mobile phones, the iPhone's SIM is only used to store customer data like phone number and billing information. The SIM on the iPhone can't be used to store contacts or other user data. You also cannot back up data to or read data from the iPhone's SIM.
