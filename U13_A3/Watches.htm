Apple Watch Series 7

The different variants of the latest Apple Watch 7 are made from 100% recycled aluminium, polished stainless steel, or brushed titanium materials. All these materials are different prices where Aluminium is the cheapest, then steel and titanium is the costliest.

It's water-resistant up to 50 meters (about 164 feet) below the surface.
